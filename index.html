What is gameHabit?

A new way to keep track of games you're playing, you want to play, and games that have not been released

Our mobile app will let you manage your gaming habit

Features

  • Create a custom playlist of new/upcoming games

  • gamehabit will track all games on your playlist

  • Get the most up-to-date information

  • Reviews, previews, and first-impressions all in one source

  • Release schedules and patch notes

  • Follow your favorite developers

  • Latest prices and trade-in values for all your games

  • Automatic updates on sales for games on your playlist

  • Check availability in your area

  • Keep track of the games your currently playing

  • Custom notes and quest trackers

  • Create your own journal

  • Connect with other gamers

  • Share your tips

  • Ask for advice

  • Buy/Sell/Trade your games
